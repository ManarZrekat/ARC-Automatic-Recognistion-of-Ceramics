Welcome to ARC!

ARC - Automatic recognition of cermaics.

The recognition model can identify four types of ceramics which are the following:
1- Juglets
2- Jugs
3- Jars
4- Bowls


Tips for taking a good picture to get the best results


Place the pottery at the center of the frame, let the camera focus and snap!


Don't take a close picture, make sure the whole pottery is shown


Future work

The goal of this project was to identify both the type and the period of the whole pottery as well as shards but due to the lack of photos we were only able to do identification by type.

For future work we're implementing identification by period on shards and whole pottery
